MemOh!

MemOh! is a mobile app prototype designed to preserve the memories of Lausanne—a city in perpetual transformation. You can capture, document and share their most treasured moments with a photo accompanied by its date and location. Immerse yourself in the city's history through the eyes of others, relive the vibrant nights spent in now-vanished bars from your youth, and don't forget to share your own memories!
